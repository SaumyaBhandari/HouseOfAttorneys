About Us

About Us

House of Attorneys is a leading law firm based in Kathmandu, Nepal, with over 35 years of experience in providing exceptional legal services. Our firm specializes in business and corporate law, civil law, and offers comprehensive legal translation and notary services. We are dedicated to delivering precise, reliable, and effective legal solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Expertise

We excel in business and corporate law, offering strategic advice and representation for companies of all sizes. Our expertise includes mergers and acquisitions, corporate governance, and compliance, ensuring that your business operates smoothly and within legal bounds.

Trial Advocacy

Our firm is well-regarded for its trial advocacy skills. We represent clients in various civil litigation matters, including disputes related to contracts, property, and other civil issues. Our goal is to achieve favorable outcomes through rigorous preparation and effective courtroom strategies.

Experienced Staff

Our team consists of highly experienced attorneys with a deep understanding of the law. Each member of our staff brings a wealth of knowledge and a commitment to providing top-notch legal services. We work collaboratively to ensure that every case is handled with the utmost care and professionalism.

Affordable Cost

At House of Attorneys, we believe in offering high-quality legal services at affordable rates. We provide transparent pricing and work with our clients to ensure that legal representation is accessible without compromising on the quality of service.

Areas of Practice

Tax Deductions

At House of Attorneys, we provide expert guidance on maximizing your tax deductions. Our team helps individuals and businesses navigate complex tax laws to ensure compliance while optimizing their financial benefits.

Civil and Criminal Cases

We offer robust legal representation in both civil and criminal matters. Our experienced attorneys are skilled in handling a wide range of cases, from personal disputes to serious criminal charges, with a focus on achieving favorable outcomes for our clients.

Contract and Property Disputes

Our firm specializes in resolving disputes related to contracts and property. We provide thorough legal analysis and representation to help clients resolve conflicts and protect their interests in property transactions and contractual agreements.

Divorce and Family Law Disputes

We handle all aspects of family law, including divorce, child custody, and support issues. Our compassionate team works to ensure that family matters are resolved efficiently and in the best interests of all parties involved.
